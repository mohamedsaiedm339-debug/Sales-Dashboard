# 📊 Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an **interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard** built entirely in **Microsoft Excel**. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, profitability, product trends, and regional insights, enabling data-driven decision-making.
---
## 🎯 Objectives
* Analyze overall sales and profit performance
* Identify top-performing and underperforming products
* Track monthly sales trends and seasonality
* Compare category-wise contributions
* Enable dynamic filtering by Year, Quarter, and Region
---
## 📊 Key Metrics
* **Total Sales:** $2,297,200.86
* **Total Profit:** $286,397.02
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 37,873
* **Average Discount:** 15.62%
* **Profit Margin:** 12.5%
---
## 📈 Key Insights
* **Technology** is the leading category, contributing ~37% of total sales
* **Furniture** and **Office Supplies** show balanced performance (~32% and ~31%)
* Sales peak in the **last quarter (Oct–Dec)**, indicating strong seasonal demand
* A small group of products contributes significantly to total revenue
* Several low-performing products generate minimal sales, suggesting potential for optimization
---
## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques Used
* **Microsoft Excel**
  * Pivot Tables
  * Pivot Charts
  * Slicers (Year, Quarter, Region)
  * Data Cleaning & Transformation
  * KPI Cards & Data Visualization
   ---
## 📂 Features

* 📌 Interactive filters (Year, Quarter, Region)
* 📊 Category-wise sales distribution (Donut Chart)
* 📅 Monthly sales trend analysis (Bar Chart)
* 🏆 Top 10 Products by Sales
* ⚠️ Bottom 10 Products analysis
---
## 🚀 How to Use

1. Download the Excel file from this repository
2. Open in Microsoft Excel (recommended: Excel 2016 or later)
3. Use slicers to filter data by:
* Year
  *  Quarter
 * Region  
4. Explore insights through interactive visuals
